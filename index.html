3. Space Risk Management Framework

Major Risk Categories
Financial Risks
  • Mission cost overruns
  • Asset loss/depreciation
  • Market volatility (space data pricing, tourism demand)
  • Launch failure insurance gaps
Operational Risks
  • Space debris collisions
  • Radiation damage
  • Communication outages
  • Propellant or power failure
Risk Mitigation Tools
  • Mission insurance pools
  • Hedging via multi-asset orbital portfolios
  • Redundant onboard systems
  • Collision avoidance modeling
  • Pre-launch risk simulations & AI reasoning reviews
